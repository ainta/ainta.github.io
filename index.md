I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. [Vladimir Kolesnikov](https://faculty.cc.gatech.edu/~vlad/). My main research areas are Zero-knowledge proof and Multi-party computation. I also work on AI-assisted vulnerability discovery.

Before Georgia Tech, I earned my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Seoul National University.

I'm also an active competitive programmer, long-time participant at IOI, ICPC, and contest platforms like Codeforces and AtCoder. Competitive programming is also where my online handle *ainta* comes from.

## News

- Our paper *[ZEE200: Zero Knowledge for Everything and Everyone @ 200 KHz](https://eprint.iacr.org/2026/828)* (w/ V. Kolesnikov and Y. Yang) was accepted to **ACM CCS 2026**.
- An AI-assisted bug-hunting agent our team has been building discovered [a critical consensus vulnerability](https://github.com/cometbft/cometbft/security/advisories/GHSA-c32p-wcqj-j677) in **CometBFT**, affecting 40+ Cosmos-based chains. [Writeup](https://qedaudit.io/blog/tachyon/).
